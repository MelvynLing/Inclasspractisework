Steps to making a peanut butter sandwich

  1. Go to your pantry.
  2. Collect main ingredients - Peanut butter and 2 slices of bread.
  3. Collect spreading knife from utensil drawer.
  4. Collect plate from cuppoard.
  5. Bring all utensils and ingredients to the counter top or table.
  6. Place the two slices of bread beside each other.
  7. Take spreading knife, and get peanut butter from jar.
  8. Spread peanut butter on bread, continue spreading until peanut butter is desired thickness on the entire slice.
  9. Place slices of bread together to form sandwich.
  10. Place sandwich on a plate.
  11. Eat your newly created Sandwich.
